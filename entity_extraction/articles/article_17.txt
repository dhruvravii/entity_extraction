Shares of India's Avenue Supermarts Ltd, which operates the DMart retail chain, fell 3.5% on Monday after the company's fourth-quarter profit missed estimates and core profit margin contracted.Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization (EBITDA) margin fell to 7.3% in the reported quarter from 8.4% in the year-ago period, the company said on Saturday.DMart's profit rose nearly 8% to 4.60 billion rupees year-on-year, but missed analysts' estimate of 5.21 billion rupees, according to Refinitiv IBES data.
